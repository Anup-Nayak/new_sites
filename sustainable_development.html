Kalp Taruh Seminar

The Kalp Taruh Seminar, conducted in association with Prajapita Brahma Kumaris Ishwariya Vishwa Vidyalaya, took place on August 16th from 5 to 7 PM at IIT Delhi. The event gathered more than 120 participants who engaged in discussions on plantations, water purification, renewable energy, sustainable yogic agriculture, and sustainable living. Following the seminar, 28 dedicated volunteers were rewarded with saplings and pots to care for. Over three months, the volunteers nurtured the saplings with guidance from the Kalp Taruh app and instructions document. Positive feedback highlighted their keen interest in planting and maintaining the saplings, inspiring the continuation of such events. The lush green condition of the saplings demonstrated effective care, while participants also gained awareness of proper plant care and its positive impact on well-being.

Rangreza

Rangreza, a Fashion Show on Sustainability, took place at Dogra Hall, offering a unique satire on the concept of sustainability in the fashion industry. The event was hosted smoothly and efficiently, creating a fun and engaging environment for all participants and the audience. The team's excellent coordination and execution of the show's various rounds enhanced the overall experience of the event. RangReza provided an innovative platform to promote sustainable fashion, encouraging attendees to rethink their approach to clothing and its impact on the environment. Through this creative endeavor, IIT Delhi's commitment to sustainability and conscious consumption was exemplified, inspiring participants to embrace more eco-friendly choices in the fashion world.

Talk Vegan Event

NSS IITD organized the "Talk Vegan" event, featuring a screening of the documentary "The Land of Ahimsa" by Climate Healers, followed by a panel discussion on "Ushering a Global Shift towards Plant-Based Systems" in collaboration with FIAPO. The event highlighted the positive impact of a vegan lifestyle in preventing animal suffering and reducing the environmental footprint, particularly in addressing climate change. Attendees had the opportunity to interact with influential personalities like Dolly Vyas-Ahuja, an Ahimsa Animal Activist and Documentary Producer, and Dr. Sailesh Rao, Founder and Executive Director of Climate Healers. The engaging panel discussion received positive responses, demonstrating attendees' active participation and interest. The event successfully delivered valuable knowledge about veganism, leaving participants informed and inspired to promote plant-based systems for a more sustainable and compassionate future.

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan Cleaning Campaign

As part of "The Clean India Campaign," IIT Delhi conducted a cleaning activity with a focus on collecting and properly disposing of single-use plastics (Sustainability and Social). The campaign received significant participation from enthusiastic volunteers who actively joined the drive (Social). Their collective efforts and dedication demonstrated the institute's commitment to the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, promoting responsible environmental practices within the campus community (Social). By addressing the issue of single-use plastics and disposing of them properly, the initiative contributed to creating a cleaner and more sustainable environment (Sustainability). IIT Delhi's proactive involvement in such campaigns showcased its dedication to fostering a greener and more conscious India.
