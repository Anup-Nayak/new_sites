Collection Drive in Collaboration with Goonj

The NSS IITD, in collaboration with Goonj, a renowned "India NGO of the year" winner, organized a successful collection drive aimed at gathering various goods, especially those no longer needed by people. The event spanned three days and involved door-to-door collections, sorting through donated items, and other tasks. The collection drive received an overwhelming response, with donations pouring in from every corner of the campus. The faculty and staff demonstrated exceptional enthusiasm and participation, contributing significantly to the drive's success. Moreover, the students' response was equally impressive, with each hostel contributing around 3-4 sacs of donated goods . The drive's comprehensive reach covered the entire campus, including all residential areas and hostels. Importantly, the collected items were directly distributed to the guards, mess workers, and other staff, ensuring that the contributions reached those in need.
